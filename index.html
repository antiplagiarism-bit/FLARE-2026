About the Conference

The Indian Library Association (ILA) is honoured to host its 72nd Annual Conference 2027 in the historic “City of Pearls,” Hyderabad, in collaboration with the University of Hyderabad, an Institution of Eminence. This premier international summit serves as a global nexus for librarians, information scientists, academicians, and technology innovators.

As the digital and physical frontiers of information continue to merge, this conference provides a vital forum to examine the shifting paradigms of our field. This landmark event will help redefine the role of libraries as catalysts for global knowledge and social progress.

The main theme of the conference is Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence 2027 (FLARE-2027). The conference focuses on a wide range of emerging and trending topics in Library and Information Science including:

  • Libraries as Drivers of Sustainable Development
  • Green Libraries
  • Artificial Intelligence in Libraries
  • Assistive Technologies & Inclusive Library Services
  • Emerging Technologies in Library Science

The sub-themes cover most of the innovative and emerging technologies shaping the future of Library and Information Science. The sessions will explore the essential contribution of libraries in anchoring the future of intellectual exchange and driving meaningful community empowerment.
