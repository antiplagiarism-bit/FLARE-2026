About the Conference

The 72nd ILA International Conference on Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence (FLARE 2027) will be hosted by the IGM Library, University of Hyderabad.

The conference will bring together librarians, researchers, academicians and industry professionals to discuss innovations, emerging technologies and the future of libraries.

Venue

IGM Library, University of Hyderabad, India

Conference Highlights

  • International Keynote Speakers
  • AI & Digital Libraries Sessions
  • Research Data Management Workshops
  • Global Participation
