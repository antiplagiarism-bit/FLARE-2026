72nd ILA International Conference

Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence (FLARE-2027)

7 – 9 January 2027 | University of Hyderabad

About Conference

The Indian Library Association (ILA) is honoured to host its 72nd Annual Conference 2027 in the historic City of Pearls in collaboration with the University of Hyderabad. This premier international summit serves as a global nexus for librarians, information scientists, academicians and technology innovators.

The conference theme Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence (FLARE-2027) focuses on emerging and trending topics including Sustainable Libraries, Artificial Intelligence, Assistive Technologies, Research Support and Digital Transformation.

Conference Themes

1. Libraries as Drivers of Sustainable Development

  • Green Libraries & Sustainable Infrastructure
  • Libraries and Sustainable Development Goals
  • Digital Preservation for Knowledge Sustainability
  • Smart Libraries and IoT

2. AI & Digital Transformation in Libraries

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Semantic Web & Linked Data
  • Blockchain Applications
  • AR/VR & Smart Libraries

3. Research Support & Scholarly Communication

  • Open Access & Open Science
  • Research Data Management
  • Institutional Repositories
  • Bibliometrics & Altmetrics

4. Assistive Technologies & Inclusive Libraries

  • Accessible Digital Content
  • Inclusive Policies
  • Multilingual Access
