72nd ILA International Conference

Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence

University of Hyderabad, India

7–9 January 2027

About the Conference

The 72nd International Conference of the Indian Library Association (ILA) brings together librarians, researchers, academicians and technology experts from across the globe to explore the future of libraries in the digital era.

Conference Objectives

  • Promote innovation in library services
  • Strengthen research support and scholarly communication
  • Discuss AI, digital libraries and data management
  • Encourage collaboration among LIS professionals

Conference Themes

  • Artificial Intelligence in Libraries
  • Digital Libraries & Institutional Repositories
  • Research Data Management
  • Open Access & Scholarly Communication
  • Library Automation & Smart Libraries
  • Knowledge Management & Information Literacy
