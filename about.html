About FLARE 2026

About the Conference

The Indian Library Association (ILA) is proud to host its 72nd annual ILA International Conference 2026 in Hyderabad, in collaboration with the Indira Gandhi Memorial Library, University of Hyderabad.

This international event brings together librarians, information scientists, educators, and technology experts from across the globe to discuss the evolving role of libraries in the digital age.

Theme: Transforming Library Services in the AI Era: Library Services, User Engagement and Community Impact.

Why Attend?

Participants will explore AI in libraries, digital transformation, sustainability, user engagement, research excellence, and the future of knowledge sharing.

Join us in Hyderabad for learning, networking, and collaboration.
