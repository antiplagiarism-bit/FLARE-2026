About the Conference

The Indian Library Association (ILA) is proud to host its 72nd annual ILA International Conference 2026 in Hyderabad, in collaboration with the Indira Gandhi Memorial Library, University of Hyderabad. This international mega event will bring together librarians, information scientists, educators, and technology experts from across the globe.

The conference is a crucial platform for discussing the evolving landscape of libraries and their pivotal role in shaping the future of knowledge dissemination and community engagement. The main theme of the conference is "Transforming Library Services in the AI Era: Library Services, User Engagement and Community Impact."

Attendees will have the opportunity to engage in discussions aimed at sharing best practices and inspiring innovative solutions to challenges facing modern libraries. The goal is to explore how libraries can adapt to the digital age while remaining essential community hubs.

Topics include AI and machine learning in libraries, user engagement, gamification, equity and social inclusion, cultural heritage preservation, sustainability, professional development, and the role of libraries during crises.

Join us in Hyderabad for an enriching experience of learning, networking, and collaboration.
