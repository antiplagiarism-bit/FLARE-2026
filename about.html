About the Conference

The Indian Library Association (ILA) is proud to host its 72nd annual ILA International Conference 2026 in Hyderabad, in collaboration with the Indira Gandhi Memorial Library, University of Hyderabad. This international mega event will bring together librarians, information scientists, educators, and technology experts from across the globe.

The conference provides a vital platform to discuss the evolving landscape of libraries and their role in knowledge dissemination and community engagement.

Theme: Transforming Library Services in the AI Era: Library Services, User Engagement and Community Impact.

Key focus areas include AI in libraries, user engagement, gamification, sustainability, social inclusion, cultural heritage preservation, and professional development for modern librarians.

Join us in Hyderabad for an enriching experience of learning, networking, and collaboration.
